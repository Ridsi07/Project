Digital Outreach & Local Search Specialist

The Treehouse is looking for a Digital Outreach & Local Search Specialist to join our growing team This person will work in conjunction with our local team, as well as our PODs. These PODs are made up of account managers, performance managers, marketing assistants, and account executives. The Local Search Specialist helps improve the overall performance of our clients' sites using various search engine optimization and marketing (SEO/SEM) strategies, focusing on link building and local search.

We are looking for a fun loving, hardworking team player who loves to learn and loves a challenge.

As a Digital Outreach & Local Search Specialist, you can expect to:

  • Create, optimize, and manage all local listings on major sites and search engines in order to improve our clients' performance on the web
  • Perform in-depth audits for local listings to ensure accuracy and work with third party platforms like Yelp, Google, Bing, Manta, etc. to update accordingly
  • Work in data aggregator platforms like Yext and Brandify to help unify listings and maintain accurate information
  • Work closely with internal teams as well as account managers for third party platforms like Google, Yext, and Yelp to help troubleshoot and correct information when needed
  • Investigate local directories and other areas of opportunity within our clients' service areas for possible link-building

What we're looking for:

  • A person eager to learn and you can learn quickly
  • A person who is excited and motivated by problem solving, not frustrated by dead-ends or challenges
  • A person who can manage their time with little structure, and musn't always be given direction
  • A person who enjoys working in a team, but who can thrive individually, as well
  • A person with previous experience using platforms like Google My Business and Yext is a bonus, but not required
